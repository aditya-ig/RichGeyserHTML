Welcome to the RICH GEYSER Video Slot – themed upon the oil rigging industry. The game features with gushing wilds re-spin and many more exciting features.

ABOUT THE GAME

RICH GEYSER is played with 10 fixed paylines. The game has 8 regular symbols that pay if three or more are lined up on adjacent reels, beginning from the leftmost position on a payline.

GUSHING WILD RE-SPINS BONUS

2 or more wild symbols in view trigger the Gushing wilds re-spin feature. Each Oil Geyser below the reels with the triggering wild, will spout Oil up to the wild position turning any position below the triggering wild symbol into a wild. The wins are awarded and the reels re-spin with the wilds moving down 1 position. Subsequent wilds achieved during the re-spin will retrigger the re-spins. Re-spins continue until all Wilds move out of the screen.

OTHER GAME INFO

  • Coinciding wins on different paylines are added together and paid to player.

  • Coin Value – Displays the theoretical stake prize of the current setting. All wins are shown in coins which should be multiplied by the coin value to convert to a Cash Prize win.

  • Bet – Shows the value of bet of the current setting. The bet size is 1 coin multiplied by the number of fixed paylines.

  • Total bet is coin value multiplied by bet per line.

  • Max Bet (Desktop ONLY) – Sets the coin value to the largest available.

  • Spin Button – Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Turbo Stop Button. Press the Turbo Stop Button to stop the reels immediately.

  • Autoplay – You can set the game to play without needing to press the spin button each time. Choose the number of rounds to auto play by pressing the Autoplay button (or by holding down the Spin Button to open the Autoplay Menu (for Mobile)). Where applicable you can choose additional conditions for when to stop Autoplay in the Game Settings. The loss limit in some game client versions prevents you from losing above set limit during your Autoplay session. To stop Autoplay manually, press the Stop Autoplay button.

  • Win – Displays the win for the current or last win paid.

  • Cash/Coin – Displays your account balance as either Cash (monetary value) or Coins (number of credits) depending on the choice of the player. The default display is Cash.

  • The conversion of Cash to Coins and vice a versa is done with 2 decimal values to achieve the closest original values, however in game, Coin conversions will always display in whole number.

RETURN TO PLAYER

  • The overall theoretical return to player is 97.36%.  

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • All winning combinations are paid left to right on a payline, with each starting at Reel 1.

  • Winnings achieved within Features are paid in addition to line combinations.

  • In the event of malfunction of the gaming hardware/software, all affected game bets and payouts are rendered void and all affected bets refunded.

  • The maximum win in the game has an upper limit equivalent to €250,000.
